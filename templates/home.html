How it works

Simple, secure, and fast

Report a Lost Item

Log lost items with category, location, and description so staff can match them quickly.

Staff Log Found Items

Campus staff record handed-in items, making them searchable for students across the system.

Claim & Verify

Submit a claim with verification details. Admins review and approve to keep it secure.

The process, step by step

From report to reunion in four simple steps.

1

Report a lost item

Submit a description — category, location, and when it was last seen.

2

Staff logs a found item

A staff member records it and it becomes visible to all students to search.

3

Student submits a claim

Found it listed? Submit a claim with proof of ownership for review.

4

Admin approves & returns

Once verified, the claim is approved and the item is marked returned.
